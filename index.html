Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Katherine Anderson, K&A Flowers began as a small passion project in a tiny studio in downtown. What started as a creative outlet quickly blossomed into one of the city's most sought-after floral design studios.

With over a decade of experience in floral design and a background in fine arts, Katherine brings a unique artistic vision to every arrangement. Our team of dedicated floral artists shares this passion, creating bespoke designs that tell your story through the language of flowers.

What Sets Us Apart

Fresh Daily Flowers

We source our blooms directly from local growers and international markets each morning to ensure peak freshness and longevity.

Hand-Tied Arrangements

Every bouquet is thoughtfully designed and meticulously hand-crafted by our skilled florists, never mass-produced.

Same-Day Delivery

We offer prompt delivery throughout the metropolitan area, ensuring your flowers arrive in perfect condition when you need them.
